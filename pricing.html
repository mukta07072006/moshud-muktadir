Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept PayPal, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is typically required upfront for new clients, with 50% deposits for larger projects.

How long does a typical project take?

Turnaround time depends on the project scope and my current workload. Basic projects typically take 1-3 days, while complex branding projects may take 1-2 weeks.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! I offer 15% discounts for non-profits and bulk orders (5+ projects). I also provide special rates for long-term collaborations.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all standard file formats (JPG, PNG, PDF, etc.) plus editable source files (AI, PSD, etc.) based on your project requirements.
